Sponsors

The Groovy project is generously sponsored by several companies:

  • Pivotal hire key project committers
  • JetBrains cover the cost of our server which runs our continuous integration server, hosts our documentation and website, and they are also offering free licenses for the TeamCity integration server and the IntelliJ IDEA development environment.
  • JFrog provide the infrastructure for deploying and hosting our snapshots and releases, thanks to the Bintray social platform for distribution, and the OSS Artifactory repository.

Sponsors are a key ingredient of the success of the Groovy project. If you consider helping the project in a way or another, please don't hesitate to contact the Groovy development team. Your help will be most appreciated.
